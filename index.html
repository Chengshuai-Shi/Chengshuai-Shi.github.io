Research Interest

I am interested in general machine learning research, and am actively exploring topics related to foundation models, especially in-context learning. My past research was focused on multi-armed bandits (MAB) and reinforcement learning (RL) problems, especially their multi-agent versions. I also have broad interests in distributed learning and federated learning.
